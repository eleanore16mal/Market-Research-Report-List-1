<p><strong>The Digital Gas Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>AMR</li><li>AMI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital gas meter market is primarily categorized into two types: Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI). AMR involves remote reading of gas consumption data, enhancing efficiency and reducing manual labor. It typically allows one-way communication. In contrast, AMI offers a comprehensive two-way communication system, enabling real-time data exchange and advanced functionalities such as demand response and outage management. Both technologies aim to improve accuracy and operational efficiency in gas metering and management.</p></p>
