<p><p>The lubricants for the cement market include several types, each with distinct properties. PAO (Polyalpha Olefin) based lubricants are known for their excellent thermal stability and low pour points, making them suitable for high-performance applications. PAG (Polyalkylene Glycol) based lubricants offer superior lubrication and moisture resistance, ideal for environmentally sensitive applications. Synthetic Hydrocarbon (SHC) based lubricants provide enhanced viscosity and stability across varying temperatures. Additionally, ‘Others’ encompass various specialty lubricants that cater to specific requirements in cement production processes.</p></p>
