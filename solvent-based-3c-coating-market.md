<p><p>The solvent-based 3C coating market encompasses various types, including thermosetting and UV curing coatings. Thermosetting coatings undergo a chemical reaction when heated, resulting in a rigid, durable finish ideal for high-performance applications. UV curing coatings, on the other hand, utilize ultraviolet light to initiate polymerization, curing rapidly and providing a strong, hard surface. Both types are utilized across industries for their protective and aesthetic properties, finding applications in automotive, electronics, and furniture manufacturing.</p></p>
