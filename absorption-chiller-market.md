<p><p>The absorption chiller market is categorized into three types based on their effectiveness and operational complexity. Single effect absorption chillers utilize a single cycle for cooling, making them simpler and less costly, suitable for smaller applications. Double effect absorption chillers operate with two cycles, offering higher efficiency and cooling capacity, ideal for larger installations. Triple effect absorption chillers maximize efficiency by employing three cycles, providing the highest cooling output, targeted towards industrial applications with significant energy demands.</p></p>
