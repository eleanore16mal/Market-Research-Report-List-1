<p><p>Dancing studio software is available in two primary market types: on-premise and web-based. In contrast, web-based software is accessed online, offering flexibility and scalability, as it eliminates the need for installations and facilitates remote access. This model typically operates on a subscription basis, enabling studios to benefit from automatic updates and cloud-based data storage.</p></p>
