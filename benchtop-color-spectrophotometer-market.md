<p><p>The benchtop color spectrophotometer market is segmented into various types, including benchtop double-beam and single-beam color spectrophotometers, as well as other specialized models. The double-beam spectrophotometer provides simultaneous measurement of the sample and a reference light path, enhancing accuracy and stability for various applications. In contrast, the single-beam model measures light from the sample only, making it simpler and often more cost-effective. Other models may include advanced or unique configurations designed for specific industrial or research applications, addressing diverse user needs.</p></p>
